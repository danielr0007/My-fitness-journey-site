Day 3 - Cardio Based

Small disciplines repeated with consistency every day, lead to great achievements gained slowly over time

Halfway done! So, very much like the day 1, this 3rd day is cardio based, and the goal is to keep your heart-rate up. The workout is just a tiny bit different, but the same concept applies: take short rests and push yourself.

Get Started

Today's Workout

25 min

eliptical

You know what to do here. Make sure you go at a nice, challenging pace. For best results, you can do short bursts of speed. For example, go superfast for 60 seconds and then slow it down. Do that several times. Look at the vids for some ideas.

3 of 12 / 1:30min rest

Squats w/ weight

They don't call it the best leg exercise for no reason! The squat will work your legs and butt at the same time. With resistance, it will promote muscle growth for the attainment of a nice, lower-body shape. Do them nice and slowly. Keep the back straight.

4 of 10 / 1min rest

Lunges w/ medball

Same as day 1. Wow, already see some progress! ;)

3 of 15 / 1min rest

Wide-legged leg press

Same as day 2, but this time, up the speed and lower the weight to keep the heart-rate up.

3 of 15 / 1min rest

Calf raises

On the same leg press machine, finish up by hitting those calfs. Make sure you do it slowly and focus on flexing those calfs! Pause for a sec on the extension, and release.
