Day 1 - Cardio Based

Dreams don't work unless you do

This is workout 1 of a 4-day sequence! This routine is designed to mold your lower body while simultaneously helping you burn calories for weight loss. You have two cardio heavy days and two resistance-training based days. Day 1 is cardio based. Follow the workouts below and make sure you keep your heart-rate up the entire time; you'll do so with low rest periods and by pushing yourself. *You two options to begin (treadmill or cycle), only do one.*

Get Started

Today's Workout

25 minutes

Treadmill

To kick off your day 1 workout, you will start with a jog on the treadmill. Don't overwork yourself, but do push enough to break a sweat. The whole point is to burn as many calories as possible by raising that heart-rate! Start with a walk and progress to a jog.

25 minutes

Cycling

If you don't like the treadmill or get tired of it, then switch to cycling. Again, keep it challenging. Make sure you break a sweat! The video to the side/below will give you ideas to keep it fun. Tip: a podcast or a movie will make those 25 min fly by!

4 of 12 / 35sec rest

Squat Jump

Half way done, good job! Now we combine cardio with resistance training by doing the squat jump. This is great for molding the legs and butt while also burning calories. Look at the video for guidance.

4 of 10 / 1min rest

Lunges w/ medball

Let's target that butt with some lunges! Grab one of the medicine balls, maybe a 10lbs one. Lunge your way from one end of the gym to the other. You should feel your legs burn (meaning it's working!). *no need to rotate or stretch your arms like the vid.
