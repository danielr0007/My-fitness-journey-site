Day 4 - Resistance Training Based

I realized the secret to success is finishing! And not just finishing, but finishing STRONG!

You did it! You're almost to the peak of the mountain, let's finish this! Like day 2, this is a resistance focused workout for muscle breakdown which then leads to growth. Make sure all your movements are slow, controlled, and focused. If the rest periods are too short, go ahead and take more time. Make sure you're using weight heavy enough to challenge you. I can already see you making progress, keep it coming!

Today's Workout

4 of 12 / 1min rest

Hip Thrust

This workout targets the glutes like no other. Get a bench and dumbbell, and in a controlled, and slow manner, use your hips to push up. At the top of the movement, squeeze those glutes, and then come back down slowly.

4 of 10 / 1:15min rest

One-legged leg press

Time to strengthen and grow those legs!. This exercise helped put some meat on my chicken legs, so I know for a fact that it works! There are several variations to target different parts of the leg. For this one, put the leg right in the middle.

3 of 15 / 1min rest

Sit ups

Same as day 2. Make sure you keep those abs tight the entire time, and keep your legs still and locked on to Something.

20 min

Stair master

Let it burn baby! No, but really, this is the last workout for this cycle, and we need to shock that muscle. The stair master is great to really feel the burn in your legs. Keep the intensity high to work on your cardio simultaneously. You got this!
