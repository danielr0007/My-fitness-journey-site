Day 2 - Resistance Training Based

If you improve by 1% everyday, within a year you'll have improved 365%

Welcome! Today, we get into resistance training. This type of training helps mold your body. The key to resistance training is to challenge yourself, meaning that, you need to grab a weight that isn't too heavy, but heavy enough to make you grimace a bit; especially on the last few repetitions. With time, as you get stronger, you up the resistance. Be sure to see the vids for guidance on good form!

Get Started

Today's Workout

4 of 12 / 1min rest

Hip Thrust

This workout targets the glutes like no other. Get a bench and dumbbell, and in a controlled, slow manner, use your hips to push up. At the top of the movement, squeeze those glutes, and then come back down slowly.

4 of 10 / 1:30min rest

One-legged leg press

Time to strengthen and grow those legs!. This exercise helped put some meat on my chicken legs, so I know for a fact that it works! There are several variations to target different parts of the leg. For this one, put the leg right in the middle.

4 of 10 / 1:30min rest

Wide-legged leg press

Now we target another set of muscles by doing the Wide-legged leg press. This time you use both legs placed at a wide stance to push. Put on more weight since you will be using both legs.

4 of 12 or 15 / 1min rest

Sit ups

Time to tone up that stomach with some plain, regular ol' sit-ups. First try doing 12, if it's too easy make it 15, and if it's still too easy, then use a medicine ball to add resistance.
